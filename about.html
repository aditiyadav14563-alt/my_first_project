About JS PVT. Industrial Traning Institute, Palhipatti Varanasi (U.P)

JS PVT. Industrial Traning Institute, located in Palhipatti, Varanasi, is a recognized private vocational training institute established in 2007.The institute is affiliated to NCVT (National Council for Vocational Traning) and function under the guideline of the Directorate General of Traning (DGT),Government of India. The institute aims to provide high-quality teachnical and skill-bassed education that prepares students for employment in various industrial sectors.

Our Mission

  • To provide excellent vocational education & industrial training
  • To prepare students for government and private sector jobs
  • To promote skill development and self-employment
  • To develop professionally trained manpower for industries

Why Choose JS ITI

  • Experienced and qualified trainers
  • Practical and industry-oriented training
  • Well-equipped labs and computer classrooms
  • Personality development and soft skills training
  • Placement assistance and career guidance
